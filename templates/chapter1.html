Chapter 1: Setting Sail - Understanding Cybersecurity Basics

Setting out on a journey, whether across the vast ocean or through the digital landscape, demands careful planning and a clear understanding of the terrain ahead. Just as a sailor wouldn’t embark on a voyage without ensuring their boat is in good condition, we shouldn’t dive into the intricate waters of cyberspace without a solid grasp of cybersecurity basics.

Consider this scenario: A small fishing boat departs for a routine outing but fails to inspect for any damage to the hull. Hours later, it finds itself taking on water far from land. Now, think about an organization that overlooks a critical vulnerability in its network. While these situations may seem different, they both underscore the serious repercussions of inadequate preparation. In this chapter, we’ll delve into the important similarities between maritime and cybersecurity readiness, highlighting the essential steps for a safe and secure journey.
